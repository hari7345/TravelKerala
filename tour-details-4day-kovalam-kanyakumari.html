Kovalam – Kanyakumari

Overview

A compact coastal escape covering Kovalam’s beaches, Trivandrum’s heritage, and Kanyakumari’s iconic sunrise and sunset at India’s southern tip.

Itinerary

DAY 1 - Arrive Trivandrum, drive to Kovalam (15 km)

Our representative welcomes you at Thiruvananthapuram Airport/Railway Station. Drive to Kovalam and check into your hotel. Spend the evening on Kovalam’s beaches. Overnight stay in Kovalam.

DAY 2 - Trivandrum city tour, overnight Kovalam

After breakfast, set out for Trivandrum. Built on seven hills and steeped in history, this seaside capital offers a rich cultural circuit. Visit Padmanabhaswamy Temple, Sree Chitra Art Gallery, Napier Museum and the Zoo. Return to Kovalam for overnight stay.

DAY 3 - Kovalam to Kanyakumari (100 km ~ 3 hrs), sightseeing

After an early breakfast, check out and drive to Kanyakumari. Check into the hotel and proceed for sightseeing: Padmanabhapuram Palace, Sucheendram Temple, Devi Kanyakumari Temple, Vivekananda Rock Memorial, Gandhi Mandapam and Triveni Sangam. Enjoy the sunset and stay overnight in Kanyakumari.

DAY 4 - Sunrise, depart to Trivandrum (100 km ~ 3 hrs)

Witness the sunrise at Kanyakumari. After breakfast, check out and drive to Thiruvananthapuram Airport/Railway Station for your onward journey.

Route spans Kerala’s southern coast from Kovalam and Trivandrum to the tip at Kanyakumari.

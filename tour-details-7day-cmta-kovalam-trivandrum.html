Overview

A comprehensive 7-day Kerala circuit: tea hills of Munnar, wildlife and spices of Thekkady, backwaters of Alleppey, followed by Kovalam’s beaches and Trivandrum’s culture.

Itinerary

DAY 1 - Cochin to Munnar (≈4 hrs), arrive and relax

Pick up from Cochin Airport/Ernakulam Railway Station and drive to Munnar (about 4 hours). At 1600 m above sea level, Munnar sits at the confluence of Mudrapuzha, Nallathanni and Kundale. En route, enjoy waterfalls and scenic viewpoints. Check in and overnight in Munnar.

DAY 2 - Munnar sightseeing (Tea Museum, Rose Garden, more)

After breakfast, explore: Tea Museum (till 5 pm; entry approx. Rs.125 adult), Floriculture Centre/Rose Garden (10 am–5 pm; ~Rs.30). Visit Eravikulam National Park (till 4 pm; entry approx. Indian adult Rs.120, child Rs.90; foreigner Rs.450). See Mattupetty Dam/Lake (9:30 am–5 pm; entry ~Rs.40; boating/horse rides as available), Kundala Lake/Dam (boating ~Rs.100–200), Photo Point and Echo Point. Overnight in Munnar. (Indicative rates; subject to change.)

DAY 3 - Munnar to Thekkady (≈3 hrs); Periyar and activities

Drive to Thekkady and check in. Visit Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary (6 am–6 pm; entry approx. Indian adult Rs.33, child Rs.5; foreign adult Rs.450, child Rs.155). Optional: Periyar boating (~Rs.225 pp; book online), jeep safari (~Rs.1500 pp), spice plantation (~Rs.100 pp), Kathakali/Kalaripayattu shows (~Rs.200 pp), elephant ride (~Rs.500 pp). Overnight in Thekkady. (Rates indicative.)

DAY 4 - Thekkady to Alleppey (≈4 hrs); explore Alleppey

After breakfast, head to Alleppey—Venice of the East—known for beaches, coir, boat races and the below-sea-level paddy fields of Kuttanad. Suggested: Alleppey Beach (lighthouse sunset), Revi Karunakaran Museum (9 am–5 pm; ~Rs.100; closed Mondays), Mullackal Market, International Coir Museum (9 am–5 pm; ~Rs.20; closed Mondays). Overnight in Alleppey. (Rates indicative.)

DAY 5 - Alleppey to Kovalam (≈4 hrs); Jatayu Park en route

After breakfast, drive to Kovalam. Optional stop: Jatayu Earth’s Center (200 m off MC Road; ~Rs.550 pp includes cable car, sculpture and temple access). At Kovalam, explore Lighthouse, Hawah and Samudra Beaches; optionally visit Sagarika Marine Research Aquarium (9 am–5 pm; ~Rs.20 adult/~Rs.10 child). Enjoy sunset; overnight in Kovalam. (Rates indicative.)

DAY 6 - Trivandrum city tour; overnight Kovalam

After breakfast, tour Thiruvananthapuram (Trivandrum)—a seaside capital built on seven hills. Visit Padmanabhaswamy Temple, Sree Chitra Art Gallery, Napier Museum and Zoo. Return to Kovalam for overnight.

DAY 7 - Depart from Trivandrum

After breakfast, check out and transfer to Trivandrum Airport/Railway Station for your onward journey—wrapping up an unforgettable Kerala holiday.

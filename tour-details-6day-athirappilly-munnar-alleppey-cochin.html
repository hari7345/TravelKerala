Athirappilly – Munnar – Alleppey – Cochin

Overview

From the roaring Athirappilly and Vazhachal waterfalls to Munnar’s tea gardens, an Alleppey houseboat stay, and heritage-filled Cochin with Cherai Beach—this 6-day trip blends nature, backwaters, and culture.

Itinerary

DAY 1 - Arrive Cochin; to Athirappilly; check in and relax

Meet at Cochin Airport/Ernakulam Railway Station and transfer to Athirappilly. Check into the hotel; leisure and overnight.

DAY 2 - Athirappilly/Vazhachal waterfalls; to Munnar (≈4 hrs)

Morning visit Athirappilly and Vazhachal waterfalls. Later, proceed to Munnar; on arrival, check into the hotel and relax. Overnight in Munnar.

DAY 3 - Munnar sightseeing (Eravikulam, Anaimudi views, dams and more)

Explore Rajamala Hills (Eravikulam National Park) with views towards Anaimudi (2,695 m; highest in South India). Visit Mattupetty Dam, Echo Point, Pothamedu View Point, Blossom Park and Tea Museum. Overnight in Munnar.

DAY 4 - Munnar to Alleppey; houseboat overnight

After breakfast, drive to Alleppey and check into your houseboat. Cruise the backwaters; spot Chinese fishing nets, paddy fields, coir making, rustic homes, temples and coconut groves. All meals onboard. Overnight on the houseboat.

DAY 5 - Alleppey to Cochin; Fort Kochi + Cherai Beach

After onboard breakfast, check out and drive to Cochin. Sightseeing: Fort Kochi, Santa Cruz Basilica, Mattancherry Palace, St. Francis Church, Jewish Synagogue, Jew Street, Folklore Museum (Thevara). Afternoon proceed to Cherai; check in and enjoy the beach. Overnight in Cherai/Cochin.

DAY 6 - Departure from Cochin

Transfer to Cochin Airport/Railway Station for your onward journey.

Munnar – Thekkady – Kumarakom – Alleppey – Kovalam – Trivandrum

Overview

An 8-day Kerala classic—tea hills of Munnar, wildlife-rich Thekkady, serene Kumarakom, an Alleppey houseboat, then beach time in Kovalam and culture in Trivandrum.

Itinerary

DAY 1 - Cochin to Munnar (≈4 hrs); arrive and relax

Arrival at Cochin Airport; assistance on arrival. Drive to Munnar with scenic waterfalls and viewpoints en route. Check into the hotel and relax. Overnight in Munnar.

DAY 2 - Munnar sightseeing (Mattupetty, Kundala, Echo Point, Eravikulam)

After breakfast, explore Munnar (1600 m). Visit Mattupetty Dam, Kundala Lake and Echo Point. In the afternoon, proceed to Rajamalai (Eravikulam National Park) to spot the Nilgiri Tahr (subject to park timings). Overnight in Munnar.

DAY 3 - Munnar to Thekkady (≈4 hrs); Periyar highlights

After breakfast, drive to Thekkady and check in. Explore the Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary (Tiger Reserve since 1978) with optional boating on Periyar Lake, spice plantation visit and elephant ride (extra cost). Overnight in Thekkady.

DAY 4 - Thekkady to Kumarakom (≈4.5 hrs); backwater hamlet

After breakfast, drive to Kumarakom and check in. In the afternoon, explore this backwater hamlet on the banks of Vembanad Lake—choose a canoe, motor boat or houseboat to soak in its charm. Overnight in Kumarakom.

DAY 5 - Kumarakom to Alleppey; houseboat (check-in 1:00 pm)

After breakfast, drive to Alleppey and board a traditionally decorated houseboat at 1:00 PM. Cruise begins with lunch; glide past Chinese fishing nets, paddy fields, coir making, rustic homes and coconut groves. Meals onboard. Overnight on the houseboat.

DAY 6 - Alleppey to Kovalam (≈4 hrs); beach time

After breakfast and houseboat checkout, drive to Kovalam and check in. Enjoy the evening at the beaches and sunset views. Overnight in Kovalam.

DAY 7 - Trivandrum city tour; overnight Kovalam

After breakfast, explore Thiruvananthapuram: Padmanabhaswamy Temple, Sree Chitra Art Gallery, Napier Museum and the Zoo. Return to Kovalam. Overnight in Kovalam.

DAY 8 - Depart from Trivandrum

Morning after breakfast, check out and drive to Trivandrum Airport for your flight back home.

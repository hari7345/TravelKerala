Calicut – Wayanad – Coorg – Mysore – Bangalore

Overview

Discover North Kerala and Karnataka highlights in 7 days—from Calicut’s heritage coast to Wayanad’s hills, onward to Coorg’s coffee country, regal Mysore and bustling Bangalore.

Itinerary

DAY 1 - Arrive Calicut; city sightseeing

Arrival at Calicut Airport and transfer to the hotel. Afternoon sightseeing: Calicut Beach, Thali Temple, Kappad Beach, Pazhassiraja Museum and Beypore Beach. Overnight in Calicut.

DAY 2 - Calicut to Wayanad; leisure

After breakfast, check out and drive to Wayanad. Check into the hotel and relax. Overnight in Wayanad.

DAY 3 - Wayanad sightseeing

After breakfast, explore Wayanad set between 700–2100 m. Visit Pookode Lake, Lakkadi, Kuruva Island and Edakkal Cave. The Panamaram, Mananthavady and Kabini rivers enrich the landscape. Overnight in Wayanad.

DAY 4 - Wayanad to Coorg; leisure

After breakfast, check out and drive to Coorg. Check into the hotel; day at leisure. Overnight in Coorg.

DAY 5 - Coorg sightseeing

After breakfast, proceed for local sightseeing in and around Coorg (suggested: Abbey Falls, Raja’s Seat, coffee estates, Dubare Elephant Camp as time permits). Overnight in Coorg.

DAY 6 - Coorg to Mysore; city highlights

After early breakfast, drive to Mysore and check in. Afternoon sightseeing: Chamarajendra Art Gallery, Mysore Zoo, Maharaja’s Palace and Brindavan Gardens. Overnight in Mysore.

DAY 7 - Mysore to Bangalore; depart

After breakfast, check out and drive to Bangalore Airport for your onward journey.
